Good Old Fashioned Pancakes

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  2. 1/2 cup teaspoons baking powder
  3. teaspoon salt
  4. tablepoon of white salt
  5. 1/4 cup milk
  6. egg

Steps

step 1

In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder,salt and sugar,make a well iun center and pour in the milk,egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.

step 2

Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat pour the batter onto the griddle,using approximatly 1/4 cup of each pancake.Brown on both side and serve hot.
